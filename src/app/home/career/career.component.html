💻 My Dev Journey (with Mild Chaos)

  • 🎓 Business Degree – Universidade de Pernambuco
    2014 – 2019 | Brazil

    Graduated in Business and went straight into the thrilling world of retail banking. Mastered the art of selling capitalization bonds and pretending cold calls were fun.

    📊
    Then the pandemic hit. I traded Excel for VS Code. No regrets.
  • 👨‍💻 Bootcamp – Let's Code / Ada
    2021 | Brazil

    Decided to give code a shot and applied to a bootcamp. Turns out 40,000 people had the same idea. 200 got in. I had no idea what a for-loop was, but they let me in anyway. Spent 6 months learning Java, HTML, and how to cry in CSS.

    🧪
    Realized I prefer debugging code over debugging clients.
  • 🧠 Software Engineer – Reachr
    2021 – 2023 | Remote

    Joined a startup. Wrote frontend that didn't look like 2009. Built a LinkedIn scraper, automated landing pages, stopped bots with CAPTCHA. Basically made the company money while Google was still indexing the homepage.

    🚀
    Went from "junior dev" to "the guy who knows why it's broken."
  • 🔧 Software Engineer – Adentis
    2022 – 2023 | Remote

    Entered the telecom world. Built batch jobs that no human ever reads but the system loves. Wrote APIs, deployed containers, drank way too much coffee.

    📦
    Learned microservices. And micro-patience.
  • 💼 Software Engineer – Natixis (BPCE)
    2023 – Present | Portugal

    Built a Value at Risk platform used by 2,000+ users (yes, with Java and Spring Boot — the adult stuff). Developed an AI news tracker that actually understands headlines (and doesn’t freak out like humans). Placed 2nd in a hackathon with a Retrieval-Augmented Generation system that answered support questions better than our docs. Currently leading a tool to monitor credit exposure breaches, with automated alerts and audit-friendly dashboards.

    🧠
    Caught between being “the AI guy” and “the backend microservices dude.” Still undecided. Still delivering.
From finance bro to fullstack dev. Still not over semicolons.
